# A5_Particle
https://lorenzockio.github.io/A5_Particle/
In this particle shader I wanted to emulate the [spitter bolt](https://youtu.be/duJ8aA_iYT8) spell from the game Noita. This is a relatively basic spell that launches an arching, pink energy sphere with a white center. The sphere loses radius over time, turning fully pink before disappearing. To do this, I apply a constant downward velocity while scaling the size of the particle with its x position. The particle is a circle with the color decided by the distance to the center. When the spell touches a surface, pink sparks burst from the sphere. To do this I added three more particles with a different “type” field. They start with an alpha of 0, and then move along with the main particle. When colliding with the top or bottom, they accelerate. The increased velocity is detected by the fragment shader, changing the alpha to 1. When they reach an even higher velocity, they stop and disappear again. For controls, I added an aiming system using the mouse. When a user holds down the mouse, the sphere stays on the left side of the screen and moves based on mouse y. Releasing the mouse fires the sphere in a direction decided with its y position. One classmate gave me positive feedback on this. It could fairly easily be turned into a minigame by adding targets.
